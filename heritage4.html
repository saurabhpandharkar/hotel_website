The Church Of Our Lady Of Immaculate Conception

Classic white Baroque architecture blended with Portuguese flavours and dash of Goan touch;
the Our Lady of the Immaculate Conception Church is an excellent structure standing proudly on the Church Square Panaji.
The church could be easily spotted amidst the colourful buildings reflecting the Portuguese roots in the land of beaches.
One can hire a cab or self drive through the neat streets of Goa to reach the beautiful church.
The massive white structure has zig-zag staircases to let you inside the churchâ€™s prayer hall.
You can offer your silent prayers to the Lady of the Immaculate Conception and also adore the architecture and design of the church,
along with the second-largest bell hanging on the top.
The top area forms a beautiful crown on the head of the church, which is often referred to as the crown of Panaji.
Make sure you visit here to capture the beauty and simplicity of Goa and its vibes.
On the street, you can buy some souvenirs to take home or munch on some Goan cuisines and street food.
You may even like to talk to the locals as they are adorable and always ready to help.
It would be a great deal for you to explore the buried history of Goa along with the evolved culture of the state.
